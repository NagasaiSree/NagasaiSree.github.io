Strawberry Spinach Salad

Strawberry Spinach Salad is a refreshing and nutritious dish featuring sweet strawberries, fresh spinach, crunchy nuts, and tangy feta cheese, all tossed in a light vinaigrette. Perfect for a quick lunch or a vibrant side, this salad offers a delicious balance of flavors and textures.

Prep-time: 10min

Cook-time: 2min

Servings: 2

Ingredients
1.2 cups fresh spinach leaves
2.1 cup sliced strawberries
3.¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
4.¼ cup sliced almonds (toasted)
5.¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
6.2 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette dressing


Instructions:


Wash and dry the spinach leaves and place them in a large salad bowl.

Add the sliced strawberries, red onion, and toasted nuts.

Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese, if using.

Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
