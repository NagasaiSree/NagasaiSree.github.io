Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Grilled Chicken


Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Grilled Chicken is a light, flavorful dish that swaps traditional pasta for spiralized zucchini, coated in fresh basil pesto and topped with juicy grilled chicken. This healthy, low-carb meal is packed with vibrant flavors and perfect for a quick, satisfying dinner.

Prep-time: 10min

Cook-time: 15min

Servings: 2

Ingredients
1.2 medium zucchinis (spiralized into noodles)
2.2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3.2 tbsp olive oil
4.1/2 cup basil pesto (homemade or store-bought)
5.1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
6.2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
7.Salt & pepper, to taste

Instructions:


Preheat grill or pan over medium heat. Brush chicken with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked. Slice into strips.

In a pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil and saut√© the zucchini noodles for 2 minutes until just tender.

Remove from heat and toss with pesto until evenly coated.

Top with sliced grilled chicken and cherry tomatoes.

Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve immediately. Enjoy!!
