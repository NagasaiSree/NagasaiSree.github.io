Avocado Toast with Egg


Avocado Toast with Egg is a simple yet delicious breakfast or snack featuring creamy mashed avocado spread over crispy toasted bread, topped with a perfectly cooked egg. Whether poached, fried, or scrambled, the egg adds protein and richness, making this dish both nutritious and satisfying. Seasoned with salt, pepper, and optional toppings like chili flakes, feta, or microgreens, it’s a versatile meal perfect for any time of day!

Prep-time: 5min

Cook-time: 5min

Servings: 1

Ingredients
1.1 slice of whole-grain or sourdough bread
2.1/2 ripe avocado
3.1 egg (poached, fried, or scrambled)
4.1/2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
5.Salt & pepper, to taste
6.Red pepper flakes or microgreens (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:


Toast the Bread: Toast the bread until golden brown and crispy.

Prepare the Avocado: Mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork, adding lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Cook the Egg: Cook the egg to your preference—poached, fried, or scrambled.

Assemble the Toast: Spread the mashed avocado on the toast, then place the cooked egg on top.

Garnish & Serve: Sprinkle with red pepper flakes, microgreens, or extra seasoning. Serve immediately!
